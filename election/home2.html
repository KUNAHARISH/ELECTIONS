About The Election

Learn about our democratic process and how you can participate in shaping our college's future.

Voting Process

Elections will be held on campus with electronic voting systems. Students must bring their college ID to vote.

House System

Sri Sivani Engineering College has two houses competing in the elections. Each house will elect 3 Presidents and 3 Vice-Presidents.

Important Dates

Nominations: July 20-25, 2025
Campaigning: July 26-31, 2025
Voting Day: August 1, 2025

Rules & Regulations

Understanding the election guidelines ensures a fair and transparent process for all.

Eligibility

All candidates must meet the following requirements:

  • Must be a full-time student in CSE or AIML department
  • Must maintain a minimum CGPA of 7.0
  • Must not have any disciplinary actions against them
  • Must be in their 2nd or 3rd year of study

Nomination Process

  • Nomination forms must be submitted by July 25, 2025
  • Each nomination requires endorsement from at least 20 students
  • Candidates must submit a brief statement of purpose (max 300 words)
  • A professional photograph must be submitted with the nomination

Campaign Guidelines

  • Campaigning is allowed only between July 26-31, 2025
  • No defamatory or derogatory remarks against other candidates
  • Campaign materials must be approved by the Election Committee
  • No campaign activities within 50 meters of the voting booths on election day
  • Maximum campaign budget: USD 200 per candidate

Voting Procedure

  • Voting will be conducted electronically on August 1, 2025
  • Students must present valid college ID to vote
  • Each student can vote for one President and one Vice-President candidate from their house
  • Results will be announced within 24 hours after voting closes

House System Rules

  • Each house (Sapphire and Ruby) will elect 3 Presidents and 3 Vice-Presidents
  • Candidates must run for positions within their assigned house only
  • The elected representatives will serve for one academic year
  • House representatives will collaborate on inter-house activities and events
